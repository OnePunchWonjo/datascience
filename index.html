Big Data is Here.

Where others just see a photo, a tweet, or a record from a doctor’s visit, you see data. You see information and answers waiting to be deciphered. Data explains where we’ve been, but you think it also tells us what lies ahead.

The field is in need of people with your passions. McKinsey and Company estimated a shortage of 140,000 to 190,000 people with deep analytical skills by 2018. And they projected a need for an additional 1.5 million managers and analysts with the know-how to use data to make effective decisions.
